Road to Success

Formula 1

It doesn't matter how confident, focused, motivated, courageous or composed you are, if you do not see your goal to the end, then it will turn out to be pointless. Be resilient, you must push through until you reach your destination. Intimidate your opponents mentally, never be intimidated by them .

Formula 2

Preparation is the key to success and there are three secrets to success. First, they have to work out exactly what they want from a certain situation, not roughly or vaguely, but specifically. Then they have to calculate the costs or sacrifices that would be necessary to achieve what they want.

Formula 3

Like a vision board, a Commitment Poster is a very effective tool that gives clarity to your dreams and feeling to your visions. The idea is to surround yourself with what you commit to yourself, of what you want to become, to remind yourself of your goals. Olympic champions use it religiously.
